The Remedy

An objective of Concerned Citizens of Longshore Lake is, in the short term, to support the management company in their effort to recover essential services, but importantly to return carefully but expeditiously to a staff employed solely by and for our community and regain the confidence in outstanding services recently forfeited.

Further, we believe that to remedy these and other ills necessitates the reconstitution of the Board of Directors and the Officers of the Longshore Lake Foundation with members who share the vision of what we were and what we can be as a community in the future. Thus, our second objective is to facilitate this through the normal annual election process.
