CONCERNED CITIZENS OF LONGSHORE LAKE

We are a grassroots organization of residents of our community Longshore Lake. We ae concerned about the direction our community has taken since the last election. The Concerned Citizens Longshore Lake (CCLL) are advocating for good governance, clear financial accountability, and honest dialog from the Board of Directors.

In addition, as residents who purchased their homes in this wonderful community of Longshore Lake, we are interested in retaining all the amenities that were in place when we moved in; active tennis program, manned access-gate, Clubhouse with dining facilities, swimming pool and an in-house professionally management.

Mission Statement

To create through resident involvement a safe, well managed, harmonious community for all to share in Longshore Lake. Seeking access to all, protection of home values through covenant adherence, financial oversight, and honesty. A return to a friendly and non-hostile environment we have enjoyed for so many years, each homeowner is entitled to the peaceful enjoyment of their homes.
